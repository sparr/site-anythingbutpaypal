Why did someone send you this link?

Sometimes people ask which online personal money transfer service to use, if not PayPal.

The answer is simple, anything but PayPal.

If you want to send and receive money with your friends, e.g. to split the bill at a restaurant or repay someone for movie tickets they bought for you, then look to the rightabove for the many alternatives available to you!
