Anything but Paypal

Sometimes people ask me which online personal money transfer service I prefer. My answer is simple, anything but PayPal.

There are a lot of sites that host horror stories from users and advertise replacements for PayPal's business and merchant services. If that's what you need, check out PayPalSucks.com, Screw-PayPal.com, PayPalWarning.com, AboutPayPal.org, or just search for "PayPal horror story". I won't bore you with mine.

If, however, what you want is to send and receive money with your friends, e.g. to split the bill at a restaurant or repay someone for movie tickets they bought for you, then look no further for the many alternatives available to you!
  • Google Wallet
  • Serve from American Express
  • Square Cash
  • Venmo (now owned by PayPal)
  • Popmoney
  • Dwolla and Current
  • clearXchange or your participating bank's compatible payment system:
    • Bank of America
    • Capital One P2P Payments
    • Chase QuickPay
    • FirstBank Person to Person Transfers
    • Frost Send Money
    • U.S. Bank Send Money
    • Wells Fargo SurePay
  • Facebook Messenger
  • Skrill iT
  • Western Union
  • Xoom (for international transfers) (now owned by PayPal)
